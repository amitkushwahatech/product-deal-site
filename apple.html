APPLE

We have positioned ourselves as a well-known Exporter of Apple. The Apple which we are providing is processed under best climatic conditions. We offer 100% Fresh and Natural Apple to the global markets.

Our Apple is widely demanded in the Food Industry. Apple offered by us are grown and ripened in a natural way and is free from all kinds of pesticides, chemicals etc. Thus our Fresh Apple are hygienic and retain their natural taste and nutrients. We offer these Fresh Apple in excellent packaging, retaining the nutritive value present in the fruit.

Apple is an edible fruit produced by an apple tree. Apple trees are cultivated worldwide. The apple is a pome (fleshy) fruit, in which the ripened ovary and surrounding tissue both become fleshy and edible.When harvested, apples are usually roundish, and some shade of red, green, or yellow in colour; they vary in size, shape, and acidity depending on the variety.
